What we do

Electrical Services in Oxfordshire

From complete rewires to EV charger installations — domestic, commercial, and everything in between.

Domestic Electrical

Full rewires, fuse board upgrades, new circuits, socket and lighting installations. We keep your home safe, compliant, and working exactly as it should.

Commercial Electrical

Office fit-outs, warehouse wiring, retail units and more. We work closely with building contractors and business owners to get the job done right, first time.

Lighting Design & Installation

Downlights, feature lighting, outdoor lighting and LED upgrades. We'll help you get the right look and feel for every room in your home or business.

EV Charger Installation

OZEV-approved home and workplace EV charger installation. We'll advise on the best charger for your vehicle and install it quickly and safely.

Testing & Inspection

EICR condition reports, landlord certificates, and pre-purchase inspections. Same-day reports available — as one happy customer found out within 48 hours.

Electrical Heating

Underfloor heating, electric radiators and heating controls. Warm, efficient solutions installed neatly and reliably — no mess, no fuss.

Solar PV Installation

Generate your own clean energy and reduce your electricity bills. We design and install solar panel systems for homes and businesses across Oxfordshire, handling everything from survey to sign-off.

Battery Storage

Make the most of your solar energy by storing what you don't use. We supply and install home battery systems that keep your lights on and your bills down — day and night.

Urgent & Emergency Callouts

Got a power outage, tripped board, or a job that can't wait? Call us directly and we'll do our best to get to you fast. No unnecessary waiting around.
